Ingredients:
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 tsp water
- 1 tsp black tea leaves

Directions:
1. Heat water in a pot
2. When it boils, add black tea leaves, and allow it to steep for 3 mins
3. Add milk and sugar and stir. Allow it to boil for 1 min.
4. Turn off the stove, and strain the tea into a cup using a strainer
